❓ Frequently Asked Questions
Why does the LPG price change every month? ▼
LPG prices are revised on the 1st of every month by oil marketing companies (HPCL, BPCL, IOC) based on international crude oil prices and exchange rates. Prices may increase or decrease depending on global market conditions.
How does subsidy get credited to my account? ▼
Under the DBTL (Direct Benefit Transfer for LPG) scheme, eligible customers pay the market price at the time of purchase. The subsidy amount is then transferred directly to the linked bank account within a few days of purchase.
Am I eligible for subsidy on 14.2 kg cylinder? ▼
Subsidy eligibility is determined by the government. PMUY (Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana) beneficiaries receive subsidy. If your annual family income is above ₹10 lakhs or if any member pays income tax, you may not be eligible. Check your eligibility on the HP Gas website or contact us.
Are there any delivery charges? ▼
No! Ambika HP Gas Agency provides FREE home delivery for all cylinder types within the service area of Nashik. No extra delivery charges are applicable.
Can I book more than one cylinder at a time? ▼
As per government rules, each household connection is eligible for a maximum of 12 subsidized cylinders per year (14.2 kg). You can book one cylinder at a time after the previous one is delivered. Commercial connections have different limits.
