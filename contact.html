Our Story

Back in 2003, when LPG delivery in Nashik was a real challenge, Mr. Ramesh Patil started a small agency with just 2 cylinders and 1 delivery van. His dream was simple — to ensure every home in Nashik had access to safe cooking gas.

Today, 22 years later, we have 15,000+ happy customers, 8 delivery vehicles, and a dedicated team that handles 200+ deliveries every single day. By embracing technology, we launched online booking, live tracking and a digital complaints system — becoming Nashik's first Ambika Ambika HP Gas Agency to do so!

We are with you — always! 🙏
